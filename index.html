SYSTEM DOCUMENTATION

Live GPS Tracking

Toggle 'SYS: LIVE GPS TRACKING' to connect to your device's physical GPS antenna. A pulsing Blue Dot will appear on the map and track your movements in real-time. Use the 'DEPLOY SENSOR AT CURRENT GPS' button to drop a marker at your exact physical coordinates.

Tactical Edit Mode & Custom Deployment

Toggle 'SYS: TACTICAL EDIT MODE' to enable map clicking. When active, clicking anywhere on the primary map (or using 'DEPLOY SENSOR AT CURRENT GPS') opens the Deployment Terminal. You can choose a marker category (Radar Blips, Marine Icons, Infrastructure), icon type, and color to deploy custom sensors.

Compass Rose & Coordinate Grid

SYS: COMPASS ROSE: Displays a glowing vector dial in the bottom-left corner. The inner needle rotates to indicate true north, while the outer radar dial rotates slowly for orientation context.
SYS: COORDINATE GRID: Overlays latitude and longitude grid lines. Spacing scales dynamically based on zoom to prevent overcrowding. Labels align with the map center crosshair to remain readable and uncluttered.

Advanced Tactical GIS Overlays

Toggle specialized transparent overlays on top of your base maps:
SYS: TOPO OVERLAY: Displays topographic contour lines to inspect local elevations.
SYS: ROADS & LABELS: Overlays road lines and town labels. Highly useful when viewing raw Satellite base maps.
SYS: MTB & OFF-ROAD: Displays mountain biking trails, dirt tracks, and multi-use routes (like the Newfoundland T'Railway) for off-road navigation.
SYS: NAUTICAL OVERLAY & HIKING TRAILS: Overlays marine buoys (OpenSeaMap) and official hiking routes.
SYS: WEATHER RADAR: Overlays live precipitation radar imagery (sourced in real-time from RainViewer).
SYS: TARGETING RETICLE: Toggles the HUD crosshair overlay centered on the primary viewport.
SYS: MAP SCALE VISIBILITY: Toggles the map distance scale ruler in the bottom left.

Default Buoys Visibility

Toggle 'SYS: DEFAULT BUOYS' to show or hide the three default preloaded buoys in Deer Lake, letting you isolate only the custom tactical buoys you deploy during operations.

Visual Modes & Map Themes

Select different basemaps from the VISUAL MODE dropdown menu. Available options include system preference, dark, light, sea (maritime), ESRI satellite, high-resolution satellite, street map, topographic map, and night vision (which applies a green monochrome phosphor filter to the entire application).

Live Cadet Tracking & Uplink

Monitor cadet positions in real-time under the LIVE CADET TRACKING dashboard panel. Remote personnel can access the Cadet Uplink at /transmit.html to register their call sign, select their vehicle/party type, specify party size, and select a custom tactical icon (radar blip, power boat, zodiac, sailboat, ship, truck, or hiker) and color to broadcast their live GPS location. Active SOS states trigger glowing red emergency warnings and audio alarms in the Command Center.

Minimaps & Expandable Sidebar

The right menu features secondary minimaps to isolate layers (Buoys, EMS, Radar, Forestry). Click the [ EXPAND << ] button at the top of the menu to widen the sidebar to 750px. This snaps the minimaps into a 2x2 grid, increases their heights, and arranges all toggles in a neat horizontal bar across the top. Click [ COLLAPSE >> ] to return to narrow view. Use [-] to roll them up, [⛶] to maximize a minimap within the sidebar (spanning both columns and hiding other panels), and [↗] to pop a minimap out to a full-screen holographic overlay. Click any minimap to instantly fly the main map view to that minimap's center.

Intel Feed

The bottom panel is the live system log. It records all system actions, errors, GPS locks, cadet transmissions, and marker deployments for audit tracking.
